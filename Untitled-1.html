The Collector's Last Acquisition

Marcus Thornfield was known throughout the city as a collector of the macabre. His mansion housed artifacts from around the world - cursed mirrors, possessed dolls, and ancient relics that whispered in forgotten tongues. But his final acquisition would prove to be his undoing.

The ornate music box arrived on a stormy October evening, its intricate silver engravings depicting scenes of death and rebirth. When wound, it played a haunting melody that seemed to call to something dark and ancient. Marcus couldn't resist its allure, not knowing that some collections are never meant to be complete...

The Lighthouse Keeper's Diary

Found among the belongings of the late lighthouse keeper Ezra Blackthorne, this diary reveals the terrifying truth behind the disappearances that plague Widow's Point. The final entry, written in what appears to be blood, speaks of ships that sail beneath the waves and passengers who should have drowned decades ago.

"The fog rolls in thicker each night, and with it come the voices of the lost. They call to me from the depths, begging for passage to shores unknown. The light grows dimmer, and I fear I can no longer distinguish between the living and the dead..."
